Above the line

Mass marketing techniques, which are primarily untargeted and aimed at establishing the brand, are included in above-the-line marketing. Everyone who has access to the medium receives the message.

Out of home branding

Advertising that is consumed outside of the home is referred to as out-of-home media, outdoor advertising. This includes advertisements encountered while ‘on the go’, such as billboards and wallscapes.

  • In-Shop Branding
  • Outdoor Branding
  • Events
  • Activation

Digital Advertising

Digital advertising is a type of marketing that businesses use to advertise their brands, goods, and services on the digital market. As a result of the digital transformation, businesses now have a wider range of options for reaching their target audiences and the market as a whole.

  • Graphic & Motion Ad
  • Content Marketing
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Influencer Marketing
